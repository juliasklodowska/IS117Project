Welcome to Butler, New Jersey!

Butler is a small suburban town in Morris County, New Jersey, United States. It is not very well-known from people outside it, so it is very important to shed light on. It is a great family-friendly place to go to and includes many parks, playgrounds, and restaurants. It has a very interesting history as well, and I recommend checking it out, and even moving in. We have an amazing community here and it is always great to see and bring in new faces. As of 2022, the population of Butler is 8,149. I strive to bring that number up to 8,500 by the end of 2023!
